ANT is a multidisciplinary group that through the integration of Art,
Neuroscience, and Technology, seeks to generate both critical and playful devices that reflect on contemporary themes.
Promoting Citizen Neuroscience, Citizen Art, and Citizen Technology.


BIOFEEDBACK provides an unique experience where you generate art from your brain waves.
On the one hand sound and on the other images.

The objective of this experience is to reflect on two contemporary issues:
Our relationship with Artificial Intelligence, as well as its algorithms and the production of individual and collective art.
